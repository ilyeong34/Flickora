Flickora Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2, 2026

This policy describes how Flickora handles data used for login, account sync, and watchlist features.

1. Information We Process

Flickora processes only the information needed to provide the app's core features:

  • TMDB request tokens and session IDs created during login.
  • TMDB account information such as username and avatar image when you sign in.
  • Guest mode state stored on your device.
  • Watchlist items stored locally on your device in guest mode.

2. How We Use Information

We use this information only to:

  • Authenticate your account and keep you signed in.
  • Show movie and TV content.
  • Save and sync your watchlist.
  • Keep guest watchlist data on the device when you are not signed in.

3. Storage

  • Session ID and guest mode state are stored locally on your device using Android DataStore.
  • Guest watchlist data is stored locally on your device using a local database.
  • When you log out, authentication data is cleared.

4. Third-Party Services

Flickora communicates with TMDB to provide login and content features. Data you provide to TMDB is also subject to TMDB's policies.

5. Data We Do Not Collect

Flickora does not use advertising, analytics, location, camera, microphone, contacts, or SMS data.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local guest watchlist data is removed when the app is deleted.
  • You can clear authentication data by logging out.
